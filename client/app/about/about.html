About us

Our Mission:

To create a thriving community of small businesses by providing creative ways to connect, share, swap and trade

Our Story:

Proquo represents the idea of exchange, 'this for that', an age old system created at a time when community was key to trading. While we have moved into a digital age, we believe community is still a fundamental part of trading. After starting as two separate innovation experiments focussed on helping Australian small businesses connect, Proquo was formally established in 2016 as a joint venture between Telstra Corporation and National Australia Bank.
